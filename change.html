So I had an idea...

A point-based reward system

And here's how it works...
  • You get points (or lose points) by doing different things:
  • You purchase an item +1 for each
  • You make an order higher than $20 +10 extra
  • You supply us with new items (more on this later) +5 for each item fufilled
  • You make it into a team (working with us) + 100 One-time
  • You are an original member +∞
  • You are the head of a team +250 Until you are not
  • You are CEO +∞
  • UNFORTUNALLY, YOU CAN ALSO LOST POINTS:
  • You have been warned once -10
  • You have been warnd 2 times - 50
  • You have been banned -10×banned time (days)
  • You have not fufilled orders -50 each time
  • You have not bought money on due date -20 + order cancelled
  • Leaving/Backing out -Bonus that you got
  • You have caused 3 errors in a day (web-sensed) -10

What you can do with points:

  • 50 Points: 10¢ off most items
  • 100 Points: 10-25% off most items
  • 200 Points: 26-50% off most items
  • 400 Points: 50-75% off most items
  • 500 Points: Sometimes free items (unless you work with us)
  • 750 Points: Have access to web code (you can edit)
  • 1000 Points: You can get a sample everytime a new item is added
